MIMMY YEHBOAH

Designer From: Ghana

Mimmy Yeboah is Ghanaian bridal fashion designer providing seamless and beautiful engagement dresses, reception dresses, prom dresses, African wears and wedding dresses. Self-taught Ghanaian designer, whose designs are influenced by the rich vibrant culture of Ghana. Mimmy describes the Mimmy Yeboah brand as a luxury classic wearable brand, specializing in women’s evening wear, day wear, African wear and bridal wears.

Mimmy Yehoab also owns GG BESPOKE.
